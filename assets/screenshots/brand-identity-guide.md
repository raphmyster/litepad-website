# Campfire — Brand Identity Guide

**Version:** 2.1 | **Last updated:** March 2026

---

## Brand Overview

Campfire is a community platform for outdoor enthusiasts. The brand should feel warm, grounded, and approachable — never corporate or overly polished. Think trail journals, not pitch decks.

## Logo Usage

- **Minimum size:** 32px height for digital, 12mm for print
- **Clear space:** 1× the height of the icon mark on all sides
- **Dark backgrounds:** Use the cream wordmark variant, never white
- **Never** rotate, stretch, recolor, or place over busy photography

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Ember | `#D4603A` | Primary actions, links, highlights |
| Pine | `#3A5A40` | Secondary buttons, success states |
| Sandstone | `#C2AE8F` | Borders, dividers, subtle backgrounds |
| Fog | `#F5F2ED` | Page backgrounds, card surfaces |
| Charcoal | `#2B2D2E` | Body text, headings |
| Smoke | `#6B7280` | Captions, helper text, metadata |

### Accessible Pairings

- **Charcoal on Fog** — 13.2:1 contrast ratio (AAA)
- **Ember on Fog** — 4.8:1 contrast ratio (AA large text only)
- **Pine on Fog** — 5.1:1 contrast ratio (AA)

## Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weight | Size |
|------|----------|--------|------|
| Heading 1 | DM Serif Display | Regular | 36 / 44 |
| Heading 2 | DM Serif Display | Regular | 28 / 36 |
| Heading 3 | Inter | Semibold | 20 / 28 |
| Body | Inter | Regular | 16 / 24 |
| Caption | Inter | Medium | 13 / 18 |

**Line height** is expressed as size / line-height in pixels.

## Spacing Scale

Based on a 4px grid: `4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96`

- **Component padding:** 16px (compact) or 24px (default)
- **Section gaps:** 64px between major content blocks
- **Card padding:** 24px on desktop, 16px on mobile

## Tone of Voice

1. **Conversational, not casual** — write like a knowledgeable friend, not a brand mascot
2. **Specific, not vague** — "3-day loop through the Cascades" beats "amazing adventures"
3. **Encouraging, not pushy** — invite participation, never guilt or pressure
